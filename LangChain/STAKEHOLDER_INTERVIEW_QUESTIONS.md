## üí° **PRO TIPS**

### **Tip 1: Show, Don't Tell**
Start with a 2-minute demo. It's 100x more effective than explaining.

### **Tip 2: Use Their Language**
When they describe scenarios, write down exact phrases they use. Use those in your prompts.

### **Tip 3: Get Specifics**
Don't accept "We serve adults with disabilities." Ask: "What ages? What types of support? What makes someone a good fit?"

### **Tip 4: Capture Stories**
Real family stories are gold. They become your test cases and demo scenarios.

### **Tip 5: Validate in Real-Time**
After they describe a program, show them how the chatbot would handle it. Iterate immediately.

### **Tip 6: Get Data to Go**
Don't rely on memory. Get documents, forms, links DURING the meeting.

### **Tip 7: Set Up Success**
End with: "We'll send you a video of the updated prototype in 24 hours. Does that work?"
